Privacy Policy

How we handle your data and protect your privacy.

Effective Date: June 27, 2025

Company: Whatsevrsocialx Pvt. Ltd.

At Whatsevr, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Info: Name, profile picture, email address, phone number.
  • User-Generated Content: Posts, offers, portfolio items, comments, and other content.
  • Usage Data: Log files, IP addresses, browser type, device information, app usage patterns.
  • Third-Party Data: We may supplement your profile with data from reliable third-party sources (online and offline).

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Personalize your experience and feed.
  • Provide targeted advertising (without revealing your personal identity).
  • Improve our app and services.
  • Send transactional or promotional messages (only with your consent).
  • Provide analytics to advertisers in an anonymized and aggregated format.

3. Sharing Your Data

We do not share personally identifiable data with advertisers without your explicit permission.

We may share anonymized insights like:

  • “Ad was seen by a user aged 25–34 in Delhi interested in design.”

We may also share data:

  • With law enforcement if required by law.
  • With service providers (hosting, analytics) under confidentiality agreements.

4. Your Controls

You can:

  • Update your profile and visibility.
  • Delete your posts.
  • Request deletion of your account and data via support@whatsevr.com

5. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Upon deletion request, data is removed from active systems within 30 days.

6. Children’s Privacy

Whatsevr is not intended for individuals under the age of 13.
